# 🤯 Mission Science BPSC: Operation Dimaag Ka Dahi

## 👥 Characters:
- 🧑‍🎓 **Ravi** – Confused but curious BPSC aspirant
- 🤓 **Chatur** – Nerdy best friend who LOVES science

## 🧠 Scene 1: Balance ka Hungama
**Ravi** is wobbling during yoga 🧘‍♂️ like a penguin.

✅ **Q1: Which part of the brain controls balance and posture?** → *Cerebellum*
